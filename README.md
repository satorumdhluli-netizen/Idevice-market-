# iDeviceMarket

**Your Refurbished iPhone Store** – iDeviceMarket is an online store for refurbished iPhones (XR → 17) and accessories like pouches, chargers, and lightning cables.

---

## Features

- Refurbished iPhones with multiple storage & condition options  
- Accessories: pouches, lightning cables, chargers  
- Storage-based pricing  
- Condition options: Excellent, Good, Fair  
- Reviews section for every product  
- First-time buyers get 30% off  
- Free courier delivery for first-time buyers in South Africa  
- Support via email and WhatsApp  
- 6-month warranty on all products  
- Clean, friendly, ultra-premium Apple-style design
